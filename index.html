Problem Statement

  • Fragmented, unstructured local markets

Solution

Market Boi makes local markets digital, secure, and efficient:

  • Verified vendors list products easily

  • Agents handle negotiation, packaging, and delivery
  • Real‑time communication keeps everyone aligned
